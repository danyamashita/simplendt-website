Why qualify your inspectors

NDT personnel qualification is a mandatory requirement in virtually every fabrication and inspection code. Level I and II inspectors can only perform examinations if qualified and recommended by a certified Level III in the applicable method.

The process includes: training and experience verification, general/specific/practical examinations, qualification report and certification recommendation to the employer.
