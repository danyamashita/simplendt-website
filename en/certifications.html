ASNT NDT Level III

Certification issued by the American Society for Nondestructive Testing. Qualifies for establishing techniques, interpreting codes and standards, and qualifying NDE personnel per SNT-TC-1A and CP-189.

Certified methods: VT, PT, MT, UT, PAUT and RT.

The ASNT Level III certification is accepted by codes such as ASME, AWS, API and ASTM. For a detailed overview of SNT-TC-1A, read the complete guide to SNT-TC-1A.

Click any badge to verify the certification directly on the ASNT website ↗

PED 2014/68/EU Approval

Approved as RTPO (Recognised Third-Party Organisation approved personnel) per Pressure Equipment Directive 2014/68/EU, Article 20. Issued by LRQA-CASL based on PCN/BINDT certification.

Qualified for non-destructive testing of permanent joints in Category III and IV pressure equipment for CE marking. Inspections carried out by SimpleNDT are accepted by European Notified Bodies without the need for requalification.

What certifications mean for your project

ASME Stamp

The ASNT Level III certification meets the SNT-TC-1A requirements for the Level 3 responsible for the personnel qualification program.

PED / CE Marking

PCN Level 2/3 certification per ISO 9712 is recognized under the European PED directive for pressure equipment inspections requiring CE marking.

Brazilian NR-13

The certifications meet the competency requirements defined in NR-13 for NDE personnel involved in the inspection of boilers, pressure vessels and pipelines.
