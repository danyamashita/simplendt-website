Dan Yamashita

Naval Engineer · NDT Level 3 · IWE

Naval Engineer M.Sc. Mechatronics IWE ASNT Level III PCN Level 3

Background

Naval Engineer from the University of São Paulo – Escola Politécnica, Master's in Mechatronics Engineering with research in ultrasonic material characterization, and certified International Welding Engineer (IWE) by the IIW.

I have been working as an NDT Level 3 since 2012, with experience in welds, castings, forgings and composite materials. Certified Level 3 by ASNT (SNT-TC-1A) and PCN (ISO 9712) in six methods: VT, PT, MT, UT, PAUT and RT. I hold ASME recognition for NDE procedures and programs, and PED 2014/68/EU approval as RTPO by a European Notified Body.

My work is to ensure the client's NDE program functions properly: procedures that meet the code, inspectors who know what they are doing, and documentation that survives any audit.

SimpleNDT exists for that — one point of contact that delivers.

Experience

Wind Energy

Quality audits at tower and component manufacturers on behalf of international OEMs in the wind energy sector. Personnel qualification and NDE procedures for large welded structures.

ASME Stamp

External Level 3 responsible for the NDE program at ASME stamp holders (U, U2, S, R, PP). Procedure development per Section V, inspector qualification per SNT-TC-1A, preparation and support for certification and recertification audits.

PED / CE Marking

Inspections as PCN Level 2 (ISO 9712) for compliance with PED 2014/68/EU. Radiography, liquid penetrant and PAUT on pressure equipment, WPS and welder qualification. Reports accepted by European Notified Bodies.

Inspection Companies

Level 3 technical support for inspection companies operating nationally and internationally. Development of specialized procedures and personnel qualification in conventional and advanced techniques.

Infrastructure & Industrial

Large-scale industrial construction and assembly projects in oil & gas, marine and infrastructure sectors.
